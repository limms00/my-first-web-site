Generation II

The second generation of Pokemon games, referred to as the Gold & Silver series in Pokemon Crystal's box blurb and instruction manual, and sometimes called the metal generation or metallic generation by older players due to the names of the paired versions, is the second set of Pokemon games released.

This generation started with Pokemon Gold and Pokemon Silver, later joined by Pokemon Crystal. It also includes the arena game Pokemon Stadium 2. This is a sequel to the Generation I games Red and Green, Blue, Red and Blue, and Yellow.
